All non-zero vectors of a FD space is eigen vector then then the transformation is scalar linear

Suppose V is of dimension $n$ and $v_1,v_2,\dots,v_n$ be a basis of the transformation. Then there exists eigen values $\lambda_1 , \lambda_2 ,\dots ,\lambda_n$ such that $T(v_i)=\lambda v_i$ for $i=1,2\dots , n$ For $v=v_1+\dots +v_n \in V$
$$T(v)=\Sigma_{i=1}^{n} \lambda_i v_i=\lambda v$$ which is only possible only if $\lambda=\lambda_1=\dots=\lambda_n$ (since $v_i$ are LI)
So $$T(v)=\lambda v ,\forall v \in V $$
$\Rightarrow$ T is scalar linear
